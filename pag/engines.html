• The 26th Workshop From Objects to Agents, WOA 2025, was co-organized by members of three units of the ENGINES project: Enrico Blanzieri (General and Program co-chair), Università degli Studi di Trento; Giovanni Ciatto (Program co-chair), Università degli Studi di Bologna; Viviana Mascardi (General co-chair), Università degli Studi di Genova.
  • The preparation of the volume "The Agents Journey -- Twenty-Five Years of Multi-agent Systems", celebrating 25 years of the Workshop From Objects to Agents, WOA 2024, involved contributors from two units of the ENGINES project (Viviana Mascardi and Andrea Omicini, Università degli Studi di Bologna). The volume is divided into four parts for a total of 14 chapters.
